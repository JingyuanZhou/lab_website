The mission of our lab

Welcome to Kaidi Yang's Lab! We work on theoretical foundations and real-world deployment of Trustworthy AI, with a focus on Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS). Our goal is to develop learning and decision-making methods that are verifiable, robust, safe, and interpretable under real-world constraints.
